Thank you for your interest in 5411 Distribution. We are confident that we are able to provide you the service that you need.

At 5411 Distribution, we ensure seamless management of your shipments—from the moment they are delivered to our warehouse until the orders are shipped out. We also handle stock management and potential returns with care and precision.

UPS is our preferred courier, but we can accommodate other options, including FedEx, DHL, USPS, or any other suggestions you may have. We offer guaranteed 2nd Day shipping within the U.S. for boutiques at a special rate. Insurance for shipped orders will be decided upon by CLIENT.

Our logistics and fulfillment services are available to CLIENT across North, Central, and South America, as needed. We work closely with our clients to customize services based on their unique requirements. For example, we will collaborate with CLIENT to establish a shipping procedure that aligns with your packaging preferences.

Before shipments are delivered to our warehouse, CLIENT must provide a detailed packing list. Upon receipt, 5411 will verify quantities against the packing list, and any discrepancies will be promptly communicated.

Core Services Included

The following services are included in the standard fee and are available to all clients as part of our comprehensive fulfillment package:

  • Receiving shipments (on pallets or boxes).
  • Verifying packing lists against received items by scanning each garment.
  • Six months of complimentary storage for wholesale, canceled orders, returns, or advance shipments.
  • Creating shipping labels for outbound orders.
  • Resolving shipment delays through prompt communication with couriers or freight forwarders.
  • Dedicated account management for your brand.
  • Fast turnarounds.
  • WMS Access: Full traceability of stock and daily shipping updates with tracking numbers.
  • Daily email summaries of shipped orders and tracking details.
